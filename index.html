Green Building

At Steadfast, "green" building means more than just a style of construction. To us, it means building with an inherent sense of environmental responsibility and providing our clients with the best solutions for their sustainability goals.

Steadfast has a proven track record of providing our clients with information and analysis to derive at the optimal balance between sustainable measures and project budget.

As partners in green construction, we're committed to the LEED® (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certification process.

Employee education is at the center of our green commitment. After instituting an internal LEED training program, many of our employees are working towards the designation of LEED accredited professionals.

Safety

Steadfast believes that everyone involved in any project should be able to go home safely at the end of the day. Job site safety is taken very seriously. We have adopted a "zero injury" policy.
The result of our accident prevention program is one of the best safety records for a general contractor of our size. We are pleased to be able to bring additional value to our clients through lower insurance rates for each project and unsurpassed safety.

Providing a safe work environment for everyone is our primary concern at Steadfast. Both, clients and workers benefit from Steadfast’s proven safety program

Our project management teams administer our Accident Prevention Program at the onset of each project, with the support of a full-time Safety Manager. Safety is not something we do in addition to the work. It's how we do the work.

03

Permit Expediting

Steadfast Development’s in house expediting department offer complete building permit, construction permit and entitlement services for retailers, developers, owners, and architects nationwide.
We procure building permits faster by simplifying the difficult and complicated building permit process. Our experts are local to your area, so we know the people and procedures that get your projects approved.

Our experience enables us to personally contact appropriate project members when crucial information is needed. This proactive approach to expediting building permits ensures proper documents are filed on time throughout the construction process.

We do continual follow up with the agencies involved to ensure that the permit process is completed within the timeline promised as part of our expediting package.

Sustainability

We approach each project with sustainability in mind, and can work with you on your own sustainability goals, providing suggestions on green construction practices and materials.
Our commitment to Green Building Design and Construction extends into all facets of corporate life at Steadfast. Preconstruction services, general contracting and construction management are just a few of the services that we offer.

Steadfast is expanding its green building expertise to serve clients with the latest in sustainable construction solutions.

As a sustainable company, we will expand our focus beyond sustainable construction to include how it makes its partners, projects, practices, and places more sustainable.
