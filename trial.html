About Us


Value through Innovation is our business philosophy. It means that we are constantly looking for better ways to build and manage projects. We identify the best emerging technologies and processes, put them to the test in the field and refine them into tools that move the science of building forwards.

Our vision is to be an environmental leader in our industry and the communities in which we work. We believe that through environmental and social leadership, we will deliver long-term value for all of our clients now and in the future. This process is a journey and not an end. We are committed to continually improving the management of our environmental responsibilities and reaching out to our communities.

Our employees are critical to our efforts. It is through their leadership and approach to our work that we can meet our commitments and fulfill our company vision. The history and culture of our company is to adopt the goals of our clients to help plan and construct each project in a truly collaborative process. Our attitude and methods are confirmed by the facts that most of our work is negotiated and the majority of that is for repeat clients. We are unique in that we can bring this personal and collaborative approach, combined with our financial capabilities and purchasing power, to the benefit of each of our clients. We are continuing to build our reputation as a service and client-oriented company who meets the ever-changing and growing needs of our clients.




David Sheedy

Sr. Project Executive

David Sheedy began his career in 1985 and gained valuable hands-on, field experience including managing his own construction company, completing residential and commercial projects as well as a qualified carpentry teacher. From 1993 to 2002, David quickly advanced from project superintendent to vice-president of a national construction management company where he was responsible for the construction of several flagship projects in Manhattan including Banana Republic at Rockefeller Center as well as other large retail stores including The Gap, Gap Kids and Old Navy.

In 2002, David joined The I-Grace Company a high end residential builder as a Senior Project Manager in the Special Projects division. David again rose through the ranks being promoted to Project Executive. Among other notable projects, he has been responsible for the full renovation of two of the most prevalent townhouse projects in Manhattan ranging from 28,000 and 45,000 square feet. During his time at I-Grace he managed over $150mm in projects.

David recently joined Steadfast Development as a Sr. Project Executive with a focus on high end residential building. With an education background in business administration and extensive experience managing all phases of a diverse range of construction projects, David has developed excellent practical, management and leadership skills. With over 25 years of experience in the field of construction, he possesses an impressive array of skills and talents that ensures the successful management and oversight of every detail of the most complex projects

Denise Broadman Sharp

Director

Denise joined Steadfast Development and Construction in 2009 as a Principle and Project Executive. Current clients include Estee Lauder, JC Penney, Limited Brands and Soul Cycle. Recently noted projects include a gut renovation of a 4 story landmark building in Soho and a fast track restoration of a historic building in Brooklyn.

Prior to joining Steadfast, Denise was President of Fisher Development, Inc. In that role, Denise oversaw field operations for the United States with a focus on the regional office in New York. She was also an Account Representative for several of Fisher Development, Inc.’s key clients.

Ms. Sharp joined FDI in 1990 as a Project Manager in the New York office and held various positions during her time there including Executive Vice President, Vice President of Special Projects and Senior Project Manager.

Ms. Sharp is noted for her experience in all facets of retail and commercial construction and for her knowledge of New York City. Over the years, she has overseen many complicated street location projects in most major U.S. cities. She graduated with a bachelor of arts from Rutgers University.

Carl Musacchia

Director

Carl Musacchia founded Steadfast Development and Construction in 2008. Current clients include Sephora, Restoration hardware, ABS partners and key residential clients. Notable projects include two major projects in Times Square and a fast track gut renovation in East Hampton, NY.

Before starting Steadfast, Mr. Musacchia was a Director at Zale Contracting, Inc. which specializes in high-end residential projects, where he managed several complete interior renovations totaling over $6 Million. Mr. Musacchia previously worked in Fisher Developments Inc.’s New York office. He joined FDI in 1994 as a Superintendent and held several positions including Project Manager, Senior Project Manager and Director. During his ten years with Fisher, Mr. Musacchia developed a specialty in building high-profile street locations.

He possesses particularly strong capabilities in the areas of effective project management, estimating and value engineering. Mr. Musacchia graduated Cum Laude from the New York Institute of Technology with a B.S. in Architectural Technology.
